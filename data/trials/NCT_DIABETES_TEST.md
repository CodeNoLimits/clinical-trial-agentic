# Clinical Trial: NCT_DIABETES_TEST

## Study Title
Test Protocol for Diabetes Type 2 Eligibility Screening

## INCLUSION CRITERIA

1. Age 18-75 years
2. Diagnosis of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
3. HbA1c between 7.0% and 10.0%
4. Currently on stable metformin therapy (≥1000mg/day) for at least 3 months
5. eGFR ≥45 mL/min/1.73m²
6. BMI between 22 and 40 kg/m²

## EXCLUSION CRITERIA

1. Type 1 Diabetes
2. Pregnant or nursing women
3. Severe renal impairment (eGFR < 30 mL/min/1.73m²)
4. Current use of insulin therapy
5. Acute cardiovascular event within 6 months
6. History of diabetic ketoacidosis
